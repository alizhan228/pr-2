Bayge

Baige is a long distance jump over rough terrain (5-15 km, and in the past more than 50 km), in which the tactical skill of the rider plays an important role. Its origin is associated with a nomadic life, the need to prepare horses for long transitions.

Kokpar

Kokpar is one of the national equestrian games. There are several variants depending on the origin of the name of the game: "blue wolf" or "blue wolf". In another version, it is said that the word "par" in the word "kokpar" comes from the words fragmentation, fragmentation. In traditional Kazakh society, Kokpar is the fat of an adult goat.

Asyk

Asyk game is a traditional game of the Kazakh people. Asyk is played both during the day and at night. During the day - sniping, at night - dexterity. Depending on the position of the yarn, it is called asyk - alshy, tayke, buk, shik, and for shooting it is made of special lead - saka,
